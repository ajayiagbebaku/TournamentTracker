Mapping the Data

Team (Object)
- TeamMembers (list<person>)
- TeamName (string)

Person (Object)
- FirstName(string)
- LastName (string)
- Email Address (string)
- CellphoneNumber (string)

Tournament (Object)
- TournamentName (string)
- Entryfee (decimal)
- EnteredTeams (List<Team>)
- Prizes (List<Prize>)
- Rounds (List<List<Matchup>>)

Prize (Object)
- PlaceNumber (int)
- PlaceName (string)
- PrizeAmount (decimal)
- PricePercentage (double)

Matchup (Object)
- Entries (List<MatchupEntry<)
- Winner (Team)
- MatchupRound (int)

MatchupEntry (Object)
- TeamCompeting (Team)
- Score (double)
- ParentMatchup (Matchup)
